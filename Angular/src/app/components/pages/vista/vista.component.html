Welcome to the Workforce Recommendation Engine!


The Workforce Recommendation Engine (WRE) is here to help you take the first step toward
better workforce opportunities. The personalized job recommendations are based on:


  • Experiences of individuals like you.
  • Current job market trends.
  • Your personal preferences.



The goal of the WRE is to provide you with the necessary information and
awareness for you to make the best decisions for yourself.
